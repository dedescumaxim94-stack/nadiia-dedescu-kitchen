Instructions

  1. Mix Ingredients: Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth. If using lemon, add the zest and juice of 1/4 lemon.
  2. Heat the Pan: Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Lightly moisten your hands with water.
  3. Shape: Shape small balls from the ricotta mixture and place them in the pan, gently flattening and shaping them with a spatula if needed.
  4. Cook: Fry for a few minutes until golden. Flip, gently press with a spatula, and cook the other side until golden.
  5. Serve: Transfer the pancakes to a plate lined with paper towels. Serve with sour cream (or Greek yogurt), jam, honey, or sweetened condensed milk.
